As a real estate broker for more than two decades, it’s been my privilege to have served hundreds of families with their real estate needs. The trust they place in me to handle one of their life’s biggest events is an honor and responsibility I take very seriously.

I have intentionally kept my real estate practice small to ensure every family that I serve receives my personal attention and the service they deserve for such an important affair in their life. This open, honest, detail-driven approach has rewarded me with a solid, time-tested reputation for delivering quality real estate services throughout Central Oregon.

Outside of my real estate practice, I am a member of the Rotary Club of Bend where I currently serve as the chairman of the Rotary Interact Club committee at Mountain View High School.

Whether helping a family buy or sell a home, or negotiating an investment property transaction on behalf of a real estate investor, my dedication to serving people with their real estate needs has only grown stronger over the years. I happily look forward to being of service to you too.
